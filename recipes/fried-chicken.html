Fried Chicken

Description

This is a triple dipped crispy fried chicken recipe anyone can follow. This chicken is perfectly seasoned with a hint of spice, but not enough to have your mouth on fire! The chicken is very crispy on the outside while being super juicy on the inside. Follow along with this super easy recipe and try it for yourself!

Ingredients

  • 1 quart vegetable oil for frying
  • 4 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 1 ½ tablespoons garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • ¼ teaspoon cumin
  • 1 ¼ cups beer, or as needed
  • 2 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (3) pound whole chicken, cut into pieces
